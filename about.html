Our Story

Established in 2011, Jumbo Quality Products Limited is a leading Kenyan company specialising in top-quality rotationally moulded water tanks under the "POA TANK" brand. Committed to excellence, we use food-grade polyethylene and advanced technology to produce durable and reliable products. We are proud to be certified by Superbrands, a recognition of our exceptional quality and brand reputation.

Beginning with water tanks, we expanded our offerings to include a variety of customised roto-moulded items, such as pit latrines and hand wash stations. In 2014, we boosted our production capacity with a new plant in Ahero, Kisumu County.

Our strategic locations in Athi River, Ahero, and Mombasa enable efficient service and delivery. As an affiliate of Jumbo Foam Mattresses Industries Ltd. and Novixa International Limited, we continue to prioritise quality and customer satisfaction as we serve our clients across Kenya.
