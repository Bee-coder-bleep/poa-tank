❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Need any assistance? Below are some of the frequently asked questions.

?

Why POA TANK?

›
The POA TANK (above-ground tanks) and the Loft Tank (in-house tanks) all come with a limited warranty of 10 years. Our water tanks are of high quality, double-layered, and have an expected lifespan of over 30+ years if correct site preparation, installation, and preservation are carried out.
?

Does the price include installation?

›
Installation is not included in the listed price. We can provide a separate quote for installation services based on your specific site requirements and location. Please contact our sales team for a detailed installation quote.
?

Does my tank have a warranty?

›
POA TANK (above-ground cylindrical water storage tanks and loft tanks) come with a limited warranty of a period of ten (10) years beginning from the date of purchase. This warranty is only applicable for black tanks. The warranty does not apply to coloured tanks or customised products. Please ask for a written warranty card during the purchase of your tank.
?

Where can I buy your products?

›
Our products are available at our branches in Athi River, Ahero, and Mombasa. You can also browse our online shop and place an order directly through this website for delivery nationwide.
?

How do I know which size tank I should purchase?

›
The size of the tank you need depends on your household size, daily water usage, and whether you rely on rainwater harvesting or municipal water supply. Our team can help you calculate your ideal tank size based on your needs. Feel free to contact us for personalized advice.
?

Do coloured tanks or other customised products have a warranty?

›
The standard 10-year warranty applies specifically to black cylindrical and loft water tanks. Coloured tanks and customised roto-moulded products do not include this warranty. We recommend requesting a written warranty card specifically for your purchase.
?

What do I need to do before my tank is delivered?

›
Ensure the delivery site is accessible by our delivery truck and that you have a firm, level base (preferably concrete) prepared to support the full weight of the filled tank. We recommend a concrete slab that is at least 2 feet wider than the diameter of the tank.
?

Do you charge for delivery?

›
Delivery charges are calculated based on the location, size of the tank, and accessibility of your site. We offer free nationwide delivery on bulk orders. Please contact our sales team to get a specific delivery quote for your location.
?

Value Added Tax (VAT)

›
All prices listed on our website are inclusive of 16% VAT as per the Kenya tax regulations. Invoices with clear VAT breakdowns are provided upon request for all purchases.
?

How will I know when my product will be delivered?

›
Once your order is placed and payment is confirmed, our dispatch team will contact you directly via WhatsApp or phone to schedule the exact delivery day and time. You will receive a notification on the day the tank is dispatched with the driver's contact details.
For any other clarifications/concerns not addressed above, please contact us, we will be more than happy to assist with your enquiry.
